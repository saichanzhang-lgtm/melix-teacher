## 第二部分：单项选择（15分，每小题1分）

> 从A、B、C、D四个选项中选出最佳答案。

**16.** —Is there ______ university near your home?
—Yes, and it's ______ famous one.
A. a; a  B. a; the  C. an; a  D. an; the
> ✅ A | ⭐⭐⭐ | 考点：冠词 — u发/juː/辅音开头→a

**17.** My cousin ______ to see me next month.
A. comes  B. came  C. is coming  D. will coming
> ✅ C | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | 考点：现在进行时表将来（next month）

**18.** —______ is it from Changping to the city center?
—About 40 kilometers.
A. How long  B. How far  C. How much  D. How often
> ✅ B | ⭐⭐⭐ | 考点：How far问距离

**19.** There ______ a football match tomorrow afternoon.
A. is going to have  B. is going to be  C. will have  D. are going to be
> ✅ B | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | 考点：There be将来时 = There is going to be

**20.** —Who taught you to swim?
—Nobody. I taught ______.
A. me  B. my  C. mine  D. myself
> ✅ D | ⭐⭐⭐ | 考点：反身代词 teach oneself

**21.** The boy is ______ to go to school by himself.
A. enough old  B. old enough  C. too old  D. very old
> ✅ B | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | 考点：enough修饰形容词后置 adj+enough

**22.** —Must I finish the work today?
—No, you ______. You can finish it tomorrow.
A. mustn't  B. can't  C. needn't  D. shouldn't
> ✅ C | ⭐⭐⭐ | 考点：must提问否定用needn't

**23.** Neither my father nor my mother ______ interested in video games.
A. are  B. is  C. be  D. were
> ✅ B | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | 考点：neither...nor就近原则，谓语与my mother一致

**24.** I have two brothers. One is a doctor, and ______ is a teacher.
A. other  B. another  C. the other  D. others
> ✅ C | ⭐⭐⭐ | 考点：两者中另一个→the other

**25.** They ______ in Changping since they moved here in 2018.
A. live  B. lived  C. are living  D. have lived
> ✅ D | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | 考点：现在完成时 since+过去时间点

**26.** —______ beautiful flowers they are!
—Yes, and they smell so nice.
A. How  B. What  C. What a  D. How a
> ✅ B | ⭐⭐⭐ | 考点：What+形容词+可数名词复数感叹句

**27.** My mother asks me ______ too much TV on school nights.
A. not watch  B. not to watch  C. don't watch  D. to not watch
> ✅ B | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | 考点：ask sb. not to do sth.

**28.** The funny story made all of us ______.
A. laugh  B. laughed  C. laughing  D. to laugh
> ✅ A | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | 考点：make sb. do 使役动词+原形

**29.** —Could you tell me ______?
—Sure. Go straight and turn left at the second crossing.
A. where is the post office  B. where the post office is  C. how can I get there  D. which is the way
> ✅ B | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | 考点：宾语从句陈述语序

**30.** I don't know if he ______ tomorrow. If he ______, I'll tell you.
A. comes; comes  B. will come; comes  C. comes; will come  D. will come; will come
> ✅ B | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | 考点：if宾从将来时 + if条件句主将从现

## 第三部分：完形填空（10分，每小题1分）

> 难度：★★★ | 改编自昌平区2023六下期末

Mr. Wang is a 60-year-old man. He lives alone in a small village near the mountains. Every morning, he ___31___ early and takes a walk in the forest.

One cold winter morning, Mr. Wang ___32___ a small bird on the ground. It looked ___33___ and couldn't fly. "Poor little thing," he said. He carefully ___34___ the bird up and took it home.

He made a warm bed for the bird and gave it some water. For the next few days, he ___35___ it carefully. Slowly, the bird became ___36___. It started to sing every morning. Mr. Wang felt very ___37___.

When spring came, the bird was strong enough to fly again. He knew he had to let it ___38___. He opened the window and the bird flew out. But to his ___39___, the bird came back the next day — and it brought two ___40___ birds with it! Mr. Wang was never alone again.

**31.** A. gets up  B. got up  C. will get up  D. is getting up
> ✅ A | every morning→一般现在时

**32.** A. finds  B. found  C. is finding  D. will find
> ✅ B | One cold winter morning→过去时

**33.** A. strong  B. weak  C. happy  D. beautiful
> ✅ B | couldn't fly→虚弱

**34.** A. picked  B. picks  C. is picking  D. picking
> ✅ A | 与found/took并列，过去时

**35.** A. looked for  B. looked at  C. looked after  D. looked up
> ✅ C | look after=照顾

**36.** A. worse  B. weaker  C. better  D. angrier
> ✅ C | started to sing→好转了

**37.** A. sad  B. worried  C. angry  D. happy
> ✅ D | 小鸟康复→开心

**38.** A. goes  B. go  C. going  D. to go
> ✅ B | let sb./sth. do 使役动词+原形

**39.** A. excitement  B. surprise  C. sadness  D. worry
> ✅ B | to one's surprise=令某人惊讶

**40.** A. another  B. other  C. others  D. the other
> ✅ B | other+名词复数=其他的

### B篇 难度：★★★ | 改编自2023海淀六下期末（降难度适配昌平）

> Many people think "small talk" — chatting about weather, sports, or TV shows — is boring. But scientists say small talk is actually very important.
>
> First, small talk helps people feel comfortable. When you meet someone new, you don't start with deep questions. You might say "Nice weather today!" These simple words can break the ice.
>
> Second, small talk can lead to real conversations. A chat about weather may turn into a talk about your favorite season, then your favorite holiday, and then your family. Before you know it, you've made a friend!
>
> Third, small talk is good for health. Studies show that people who talk with others regularly — even just short greetings — feel happier and less lonely. A simple "Good morning" to your neighbor can brighten both of your days.
>
> So next time someone talks about the weather, don't just say "yes" and walk away. Use it as a chance to connect!

## 第五部分：词汇与句型（20分）

### 六、用括号内所给单词的适当形式填空。（10分，每空1分）

**51.** My father enjoys ______ (fish) on weekends.

**52.** Tuesday is the ______ (three) day of the week.
> ✅ third | 序数词 three→third

**53.** She ______ (buy) a gift for her mother last Sunday.
> ✅ bought | 过去时 buy→bought

**54.** Please speak ______ (loud). I can't hear you.
> ✅ loudly | 副词修饰动词

**55.** This book is ______ (interesting) than that one.
> ✅ more interesting | 多音节比较级

**56.** We should brush our ______ (tooth) twice a day.
> ✅ teeth | 不规则复数 tooth→teeth

**57.** It's ______ (danger) to swim in the river alone.
> ✅ dangerous | 名词→形容词

**58.** The children enjoyed ______ (they) at the party.
> ✅ themselves | enjoy oneself

**59.** There are two ______ (shelf) in my study.
> ✅ shelves | f→ves shelf→shelves

> ✅ hasn't finished | 现在完成时+yet

### 七、按要求完成句子。（10分，每小题2分）

**61.** He read a storybook last night.（改为否定句）
> ✅ He didn't read a storybook last night.
> 考点：过去时否定 didn't+原形

**62.** There are some trees behind the house.（改为一般疑问句）
> ✅ Are there any trees behind the house?
> 考点：There be疑问句+some→any

**63.** The boy is playing basketball now.（对划线部分提问→playing basketball）
> ✅ What is the boy doing now?
> 考点：现在进行时特殊疑问句

**64.** She went to Beijing by train.（对划线部分提问→by train）
> ✅ How did she go to Beijing?
> 考点：交通方式→How

**65.** Tom is 1.65m tall. Jack is 1.65m tall, too.（合并为一句）
> ✅ Tom is as tall as Jack.
> 考点：同级比较 as...as

## 第六部分：书面表达（15分）

> **题目：My Favorite Teacher**
>
> 要点：
> 1. 你最喜欢的老师是谁？（姓名+科目）
> 2. 他/她长什么样？
> 3. 为什么喜欢？（至少两个理由）
> 4. 你想对他/她说什么？
>
> 词数：60-80词

### 参考范文

My favorite teacher is Miss Zhang. She teaches us English. She is tall and thin with long black hair. She always smiles.

I like her for two reasons. First, her classes are very interesting. She often tells us English stories. Second, she is kind and patient. When I make mistakes, she encourages me to try again.

I want to say: "Thank you, Miss Zhang! You make English fun!"
